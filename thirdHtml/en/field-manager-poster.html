Core Modules

Six Pillars of
Field Management

Workforce Management
Real-time visibility into all on-site personnel. Assign tasks, monitor status, and coordinate teams from a unified dispatch board with role-based access control.
Fleet Operations
Track, dispatch, and optimize your field fleet in real time. Monitor vehicle health, fuel levels, and route efficiency with live GPS integration.
Facility Oversight
Comprehensive monitoring of field sites and infrastructure. Track access, safety compliance, equipment status, and environmental conditions at a glance.
Trajectory Tracking
Visualize movement patterns and historical paths of personnel and vehicles. Geofencing alerts, playback analysis, and predictive route optimization.
AI Vision Alerting
Leverage vision foundation models to analyze live camera feeds in real time. Automatically detect safety hazards, compliance violations, and anomalies with instant alert triggers.
IM Channel Integration
Push field operations updates directly to enterprise IM channels. Remote managers stay informed with real-time notifications, command dispatch, and instant two-way communication.
