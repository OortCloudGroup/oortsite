Technology that delivers outcomes, not just tools

OortCloud is an enterprise technology company building intelligent cloud platforms and AI agents that transform how organizations operate. We believe the future belongs to companies that deploy AI to deliver completed work — not software that requires it.

Our Mission

To empower enterprises with intelligent cloud infrastructure and AI-driven automation that replaces complexity with clarity, turning routine operations into automated outcomes.

Founded in 2019 in Shenzhen, China, OortCloud has grown from a private cloud solutions provider into a comprehensive enterprise AI platform company. We serve government agencies, public safety departments, healthcare systems, educational institutions, and commercial enterprises across the globe.

Our journey

From private cloud solutions to a global enterprise AI platform

2025

The Future of Enterprise AI

Pioneering the shift from "AI as a tool" to "AI as a service" — delivering completed operational outcomes through autonomous enterprise agents.

2024

Global Expansion

Launched OORT.SH developer platform. Expanded internationally with localized products for overseas markets. Surpassed 150 million cumulative users.

2023

AI Agent Platform

Launched OORT.AI with nine specialized enterprise agents. Introduced VLStream visual AI platform under MIT open-source license. Recognized as a National-level Technology SME.

2022

Platform Expansion

Achieved AAA credit rating. Expanded product suite to 300+ enterprise applications. Certified with ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 standards.

2021

Workup Super App Launch

Released the all-in-one enterprise workspace integrating messaging, email, approval, drive, and classroom. Strategic partnerships with Huawei and CETC established.

2020

First Enterprise Deployments

Launched core cloud platform and command dispatch solutions. Secured initial enterprise and government clients with private deployment architecture.

2019

Founded in Shenzhen

OortCloud established with a focus on private cloud infrastructure and intelligent application development for enterprise and public safety sectors.

What guides us

The principles behind every product we build

01

Outcomes Over Tools

We build AI that delivers completed work, not software that creates more work. Our goal is to reduce operational overhead, not add layers of complexity.

02

Security by Default

Enterprise data sovereignty is non-negotiable. Every product supports private deployment, end-to-end encryption, and compliance with global standards.

03

Integration First

Isolated tools create silos. We build products that work together seamlessly — sharing context, data, and workflow across the entire platform.

04

Enterprise Adaptability

Every organization is different. Our platforms adapt to complex permission systems, existing infrastructure, and unique operational requirements.

05

Open Innovation

We contribute to the open-source community through VLStream (MIT licensed) and OORT.SH, believing that open collaboration accelerates innovation for everyone.

06

Global Thinking

Built in Shenzhen, designed for the world. We create technology that transcends borders, languages, and industries — serving enterprises wherever they operate.
